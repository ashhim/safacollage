    About Us

    Welcome to Safa College of Arts and Science, where excellence meets innovation in education. The college is one among the leading institutions in the self-financing sector, affiliated with the University of Calicut and run by Orphans Educational Charitable Trust Edayur. Established with a vision to impart holistic education by honorable Sir V. P Kunhimoideenkutty, it stands as a beacon of academic excellence, fostering a vibrant community of learners driven by curiosity and creativity.
